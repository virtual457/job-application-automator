Want to customize the LaTeX template?

1. FONTS
   Add to preamble:
   \usepackage{helvet}          % Helvetica
   \renewcommand{\familydefault}{\sfdefault}

2. COLORS
   Add to preamble:
   \usepackage{xcolor}
   \definecolor{myblue}{RGB}{0,102,204}
   
   Use in sections:
   \titleformat{\section}{\large\bfseries\color{myblue}}...

3. SPACING
   Adjust margins:
   \usepackage[top=0.3in, bottom=0.3in, left=0.6in, right=0.6in]{geometry}
   
   Adjust section spacing:
   \titlespacing*{\section}{0pt}{8pt}{5pt}

4. BULLET POINTS
   Customize:
   \setlist[itemize]{noitemsep, topsep=3pt, leftmargin=1.5em}
